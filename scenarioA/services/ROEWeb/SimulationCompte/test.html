My Organization(s)

You currently have no organizations registered to your account.

Options

  • Register a New Organization

    Submit a new request to register the organization you represent to the ROE Web program. Please ensure the organization is not already registered and that you are authorized to act as the account administrator (primary officer).

  • Replace/become the Primary Officer of an existing organization

    Submit a request to become the new account administrator (primary officer) for an already registered organization. Before submitting a request, ensure you are authorized to replace the current primary officer.

  • Become the Representative of a Registered Organization.

    To become a representative of a registered organization, you will need to provide your user reference number (URN) to the primary officer or authorized representative of that organization. Your URN is a unique alphanumeric code that can be found on top of this page. It will be used by the primary officer or the authorized representative to send you an invitation to join the organization.
