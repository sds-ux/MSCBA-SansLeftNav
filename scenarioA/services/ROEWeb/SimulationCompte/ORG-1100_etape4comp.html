Organization 


Submit a request to register the organization you represent to the Record of Employment on the Web (ROE Web). Please ensure the organization is not already registered and that you have the authority to act as the primary officer.

Are your ROEs submitted by a different Organization?

You can regain control of your Business Number by registering your organization. This applies when a different organization uses your Business Number (BN) to submit ROEs on your organization’s behalf. Follow the registration steps until you submit your BN to confirm your organization. One of our agents will get in touch with you within 10 working days. They will transfer control or your BN back to you.


Primary Officer 


Submit a request to become the new primary officer for an already registered organization. Before submitting a request, ensure you have the authority to replace the current primary officer.

Become a Representative 


To become a representative of a registered organization, you need to give your user reference number (URN) to the primary officer of that organization. Your URN is the alphanumeric code next to your name at the top of this page. The primary officer will use your URN to send you an invitation to join the organization.
